Trezor Suite: Understanding Intellectual Property Protection

Trezor's product ecosystem

Trezor Suite provides users with secure management of their digital currencies and facilitates the integration of Trezor hardware wallets with various devices. The software is vital in ensuring the security and accessibility of digital assets, but like any piece of technology, it is subject to copyright laws.

Legal protection

Trezor Suite, the code and design elements are protected under copyright law, meaning that unauthorized copying, distribution, or modification of the software is prohibited. The creators, SatoshiLabs, hold exclusive rights to the distribution and use of the software unless they provide explicit permission for others to engage in specific actions, such as modifications or redistribution.

Open-source license

This means that the software's source code is publicly available for anyone to inspect, modify, and distribute, provided they adhere to the terms of the specific open-source license it is distributed under (usually a license such as MIT or GPL).
